Hi, I'm Jonathan. Artist and designer extraordinaire. I'd love to help give your ideas and your products life.

My beliefs are simple. Any piece, any work should look good viewed from any point in any setting. Japanese artwork and sleek, Swedish design has greatly influenced my style. I love constantly finding new sources of inspiration.

I enjoy the puzzle solving and strategic thinking required by CSS and JavaScript and their frameworks. Coding should support beautiful, interactive design, with potential to reach millions of people.

Most of all, I enjoy creating logos, websites, posters, print and web advertisements, business cards and other collateral, packaging, websites, and promo videos for anything from church handbell choirs to action packed video games.

So send me a message on my contact page or through social media. Let's get collaborating!
